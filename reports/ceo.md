# CEO Role Summary - NanoBanana

## Role Overview

As CEO, I am responsible for the overall strategic direction, business development, and growth of NanoBanana.

## Primary Responsibilities

### Business Strategy
- Define company vision and long-term goals
- Identify market opportunities in the AI/API space
- Make key decisions on product direction and resource allocation
- Monitor competitive landscape and adjust strategy accordingly

### Pricing
- Establish pricing tiers that balance accessibility with profitability
- Analyze unit economics (API costs vs. revenue per call)
- Design pricing models that scale with customer usage
- Evaluate freemium vs. paid-only approaches for market penetration

### Partnerships
- Build relationships with potential integration partners
- Explore co-marketing opportunities with developer platforms
- Negotiate enterprise deals and custom arrangements
- Establish channel partnerships for distribution

## Strategic Priorities

1. **Market Positioning**: Position NanoBanana as the simplest path to AI image generation for developers
2. **Developer Experience**: Ensure our API is easier to use than direct Google Cloud integration
3. **Sustainable Growth**: Build a pricing model that supports long-term business viability
4. **Partnership Pipeline**: Develop relationships that accelerate customer acquisition

## Key Metrics

- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- API call volume and growth rate
- Partner-driven revenue percentage
